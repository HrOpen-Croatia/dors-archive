DORS/CLUC

Archive of the Croatian free software conference, 2013–2026.

The live website at dorscluc.org has been retired. This is a static snapshot of every public edition the conference ran.

  • 2026

    The final edition. Call for speakers, sponsors, and the last programme before the conference wound down.

  • 2023

    Thirtieth DORS/CLUC, hosted at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computing in Zagreb — the post-pandemic return.

  • 2022

    Flossing together since 1994. Full schedule, speakers, sponsors, and session videos.

  • 2020

    The virtual edition — Open Systems Days and Croatian Linux Users' Conference held online.

  • 2019

    26th edition. Talks, workshops, and the social programme around open systems in Croatia.

  • 2018

    25th edition — the silver anniversary. Speakers from across the free-software world.

  • 2017

    24th Open Systems Days. Schedule, speakers, and the annual hack-focused gathering.

  • 2016

    23rd edition. First year on the Django-backed site; full talk and speaker programme.

  • 2015

    22nd Open Systems Days, organised by HULK and HrOpen with the FER faculty.

  • 2014

    21st edition — the original WordPress-era site with talks, sponsors, and registration archives.

  • 2013

    20th edition — the oldest surviving snapshot in this archive, covering speakers, schedule, and call-for-papers.

Evergreen pages (Code of Conduct, past-conferences index, contact) live at /main/.
